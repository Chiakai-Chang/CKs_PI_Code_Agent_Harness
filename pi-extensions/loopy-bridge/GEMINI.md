# Loopy Bridge

This extension bridges [Forward-Future/loopy](https://github.com/Forward-Future/loopy) into the Pi Harness.

## Key Concepts
*   **Loop Engineering**: Replaces open-ended prompts with bounded feedback playbooks.
*   **Loop Doctor**: Audits existing loops for safety, weak assertions, and runaway risks.
*   **Run Receipts**: Outputs structured evidence logs of what the loop accomplished, what it learned, and why it stopped.
