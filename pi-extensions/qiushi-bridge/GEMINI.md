# Qiushi Bridge

This extension bridges [HughYau/qiushi-skill](https://github.com/HughYau/qiushi-skill) into the Pi Harness.

## Core Philosophical Baseline
*   **Contradiction Analysis (`qiushi-contradiction`)**: Identify competing forces, isolate the principal contradiction, classify its nature (antagonistic vs non-antagonistic), and prioritize action.
*   **Practice-Cognition Loop (`qiushi-practice`)**: Standardize the trial-and-error cycle to prevent repeating known errors.
*   **Concentrated Attention (`qiushi-focus`)**: Focus tokens and compute on resolving the single most critical bottleneck.
