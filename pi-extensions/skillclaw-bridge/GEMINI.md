# SkillClaw Bridge

This extension bridges [AMAP-ML/SkillClaw](https://github.com/AMAP-ML/SkillClaw) into the Pi Harness.

## Core Philosophical Baseline
*   **Collective Learning**: Compound experience across sessions, users, and devices.
*   **Evolver Engine**: Summarize agent rollouts, aggregate patterns, decide on skill actions, self-validate with dry runs, and maintain version ledger.
